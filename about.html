Our Story

Founded in 2023, DataInsight Consulting was born from a passion for transforming raw data into meaningful business insights. Our team of experienced data professionals came together with a shared vision: to make data analytics accessible and actionable for businesses of all sizes.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into a trusted partner for organizations across multiple industries, helping them navigate the complex world of data and emerge with clear, strategic direction.
